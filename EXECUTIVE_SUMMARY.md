# Axioms Credit Constitution — Executive Summary

## Purpose
AXIOMS is a transparent, deterministic credit scoring standard designed to
replace opaque legacy scoring systems.

## Key Benefits
- Transparent scoring rules  
- Deterministic outputs  
- Publicly auditable logic  
- Enterprise-ready API  
- Optional blockchain verification  

## How It Works
AXIOMS uses:
- a constitutional ruleset  
- versioned factor configs  
- independent factor engines  
- WAD precision math  

This ensures scores are predictable and reproducible.

## Why It Matters
Enterprises, regulators, and users gain:
- clarity  
- fairness  
- trust  
- auditability  

AXIOMS eliminates black-box scoring.

## Summary
AXIOMS establishes a modern, transparent, and deterministic credit scoring
framework built for enterprise adoption and regulatory compliance.
